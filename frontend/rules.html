SERVER RULES

Read and follow all rules. Ignorance is not an excuse.

👥 GUNPLAY OVER ROLEPLAY

• Shootouts require IC reasons and narrative build-up
• Combat is not a default response
• Always attempt de-escalation through dialogue first

🔒 HACKING / EXPLOITING

• Report broken mechanics immediately
• No combat logging
• No money/item duplication or economic glitches
• No transferring items between characters

🧠 METAGAMING

• No using info your character hasn't learned in-game
• Includes streams, Discord, forums, etc.
• No third-party comms while connected

🎭 NO CHARACTER BAITING

• No provoking armed characters without IC reason
• No burnouts in front of officers
• No circling/stalking players in vehicles

🔁 CHARACTER BLEEDING

• Information from one character cannot transfer to another
• Police SOPs cannot be used by criminals

👑 CORRUPTION ROLEPLAY

• Bribery and corruption RP requires approval
• Unauthorized corruption RP results in removal
